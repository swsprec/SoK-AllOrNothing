Why am I receiving connection attempts from Northeastern University?

These connections are part of computer science research projects with the goal of making the Internet a safer place for all who use it. This research involved making single connections with websites and interacting very breifly. This allows scientists to measure certain phenomena that could be harmful to patrons of the Internet.

We are NOT and will NEVER exploit any security problems or vulnerabilites, guess passwords, or change device configurations. We only receive data that is publicly visible to anyone who connects to a webserver.

Can I opt-out of these measurements?

This research helps the scientific community make the Internet safer for everyone. If you wish to opt-out, you can configure your firewall to drop traffic from the IP addresses currently being used for measurements: N/A (during COVID-19). If you have further questions, or wish to have your website added to our blacklist, please contact: DataExStudy@iseclab.org
